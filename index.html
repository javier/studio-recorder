Output: webcam-[timestamp].webm + screen-[timestamp].webm
On Chrome/Edge you can pick a save folder. Other browsers will download to your default location.
The browser will ask which screen or window to share when you click Start.
Tick "Share audio" in the browser picker to capture system sound with the screen.

If recording fails on file://, serve over localhost instead:
cd /path/to/folder && python3 -m http.server 8080
Then open http://localhost:8080/studio-recorder.html
